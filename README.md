# Fooda

AI 智能卡路里追蹤 App — 拍照即可識別食物熱量，搭配健康目標管理每日營養攝取。

## 功能
- **拍照辨識**：相機拍照或相簿選圖，AI 自動分析食物種類與熱量
- **手動輸入**：手動新增飲食紀錄
- **每日儀表板**：顯示熱量、蛋白質、碳水、脂肪的攝取進度
- **歷史查詢**：依日期查看過去所有飲食紀錄
- **分析報表**：視覺化開銷分佈
- **個人目標**：設定性別、體重、每日熱量目標

## 技術棧
- Flutter（iOS / Android）
- AI 多模態影像辨識
- Firebase（Auth + Firestore）

## English

An AI calorie tracker. Snap a photo, get the food and its calories; manage daily nutrition against your health goals.

### Features
- **Photo recognition**: take a photo or pick from album; AI identifies the food and its calories
- **Manual entry**: add meals by hand
- **Daily dashboard**: progress bars for calories, protein, carbs, and fat
- **History**: browse past meals by date
- **Reports**: visualized intake breakdown
- **Personal goals**: set gender, weight, and daily calorie target

### Tech stack
- Flutter (iOS / Android)
- Multimodal AI image recognition
- Firebase (Auth + Firestore)
